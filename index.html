STATUARIO a platformer by parshav kothary

Statuario is a minimalistic 2D puzzle-platformer with a heavy focus on movement, physics, and game-feel. It aims to create a relaxing atmosphere to enhance the player's focus and encourage them to try harder. It will be released soon on iOS, Android, and Steam.

A snappy, satisfying, movement system aims to make the player feel engaged and in total control.

Levels vary between themes. Some require logical thinking while others require precise movement.

Never run out of levels with an in-game level creator and a sharing forum!
